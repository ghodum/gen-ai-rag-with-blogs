Solution Street makes Inc 5000 list for the 3rd Consecutive Year
For the third year in a row, Solution Street makes Inc 5000 list of fastest, growing private companies. Solution Street was ranked number 2076. Over the last three years, Solution Street has increased by 193% and 15 jobs have been added. According to Arthur Frankel, managing partner of Solution Street, he credits his hardworking staff and clients to the company's success. Solution Street is a software consulting company located in northern Virginia's high-tech corridor.
